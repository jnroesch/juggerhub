# Code of Conduct

JuggerHub is maintained by one person in their spare time. This isn't a large
organization with a moderation team — it's a small open-source project. This
document just sets out what's expected of people taking part **in this
repository**.

## Scope

This applies to activity **in this repository**: issues, pull requests, code
review, commit messages, and any other comments here. It does **not** cover what
anyone does elsewhere — other platforms, private messages, or the wider world are
outside what I can see or act on.

## What's expected

Keep it simple:

- **Be respectful and constructive.** Assume good faith. Critique ideas and code,
  not people.
- **Stay on topic.** Issues and pull requests are for building JuggerHub.
- **No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or spam.**
- **Don't post other people's private information.**

## Enforcement

I maintain this repo alone, so enforcement is deliberately simple. If something
here breaks the expectations above, I may — at my discretion and without a formal
process — edit or delete the content, close the issue or pull request, and/or
block the account from the repository.

If you see something that needs attention, or you're on the receiving end of it,
email me at **j.n.roesch@googlemail.com**. I'll look into it when I can. I can
only act on things that happen inside this repository.

That's it. Be decent, and let's build something good for the Jugger community.
